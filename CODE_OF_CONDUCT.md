# Code Of Conduct

Dude contributors are expected to keep collaboration professional, specific, and evidence-based.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and respectful.
- Critique code, docs, and claims rather than people.
- Use source-backed reasoning for Singapore data, policy, licensing, and compliance discussions.
- Respect maintainer decisions about scope, licensing risk, and unsupported advisory claims.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when proposing vendor, registry, partner, or paid-data integrations.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Pressuring maintainers to ship claims or integrations that lack provenance, freshness, licensing review, or safety boundaries.
- Submitting intentionally misleading data, fabricated citations, or hidden commercial promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block accounts, or reject contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations can result in a ban from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct or safety concerns to the maintainer team using the security and governance contacts in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md). If the concern involves a maintainer, report it to another listed maintainer or project owner.
