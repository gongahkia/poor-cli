## Buyer Eligibility Context

For SME buyers applying for PSG, the current general EnterpriseSG posture is:

- business entity registered and operating in Singapore;
- at least 30% local equity held by Singapore citizens or permanent residents, directly or indirectly by ultimate individual ownership;
- group annual sales turnover not above S$100 million, or group employment size not above 200;
- the IT solution, equipment, lease, or subscription must be used in Singapore;
- no payment, deposit, contract, or purchase commitment before PSG application submission;
- applicant applies directly through the Business Grants Portal using Corppass;
- one UEN per application, with no group applications.

This buyer context matters for sales enablement, but it does not make Dude pre-approved. Dude still needs vendor pre-approval before buyers can select it as a PSG-supported solution.

## Vendor Pre-Approval Requirements

The relevant first path is IMDA Pre-Approved Solutions under SMEs Go Digital, unless a sector agency later identifies a better fit.

Current gating criteria to clear before application:

- Singapore-registered ICM vendor.
- Company incorporated for at least 18 months.
- At least five SME customers from the target sector using the solution for at least six months and still using it.
- At least five SME customers satisfied with the vendor's service.
- Latest financial year has positive net equity.
- Latest financial year current ratio is at least 1.
- Satisfactory government-agency track record and no suspension from pre-approval consideration.
- Fully operational professional website with solution details.
- At least five SME customers satisfied with solution price and quality.
- At least five SME customers reporting at least 15% productivity gain from the solution, or at least 20% if applying under the built-environment sector.
- The solution satisfies the relevant category checklist.
- Support coverage includes at least 8 hours x 5 weekdays of post-sales support by on-site or teleconference and 24/7 reachability by email or contact form.

## Required Materials

### Company Packet

- UEN, legal entity name, ACRA Bizfile, registered address, ownership/shareholding summary, and authorised signatory.
- Latest financial statements or management accounts showing net equity and current ratio.
- Website URL with solution description, support route, privacy/DPO contact, security posture, and non-advice limits.
- Government track-record statement and any prior grant, public-sector, or procurement interactions.
- Support operating model covering local hours, escalation route, customer-success owner, incident route, and service-contact evidence.

### Product Packet

- Solution category recommendation and selected category checklist.
- Feature matrix for corp-services CDD, UEN search, dossier evidence, analyst memo, exports, provenance/freshness/gaps/limits, and audit-ready artifacts.
- Demo script, screenshots, sample outputs, and technical architecture.
- Data-source limits covering ACRA, OneMap, URA, PDPA, DPA, SOC 2 roadmap, MAS outsourcing readiness, and non-advice clauses.
- Security and privacy materials linked from [hosted onboarding](./product/hosted-onboarding.md).

### Customer Evidence Packet

- Five qualifying SME references from the target sector, excluding subsidiaries and affiliated companies.
- Proof each SME used Dude for at least six months and is still using it.
- Customer satisfaction survey responses for service, solution price, and solution quality.
- Productivity-gain evidence showing at least 15% improvement tied to the selected workflow.
- Permission status for public reference, anonymized case study, or private reviewer-only evidence.

### Pricing And Quotation Packet

- PSG package name, qualifying subscription/licence term, and feature scope.
- Cost breakdown that separates software subscription, professional services, training, optional add-ons, and non-claimable administrative items.
- Quotation template addressed to the customer's registered company name as shown in ACRA Bizfile.
- Annex/category mapping so package items match the selected pre-approved solution scope.
- Claim-support instructions for usage reports, implementation proof, invoice, and payment evidence.
