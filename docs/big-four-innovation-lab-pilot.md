# Big-4 Innovation Lab Pilot Packet

This packet scopes a credibility-oriented pilot with a Big-4 innovation, AI, digital, or client-experience team. It avoids overclaiming licensed compliance coverage and keeps the ask narrow.

Observed on 2026-05-17.

## Success Definition

- Innovation lab or digital transformation targets and warm-intro paths are identified.
- Pilot scope is bounded to public-data evidence and non-advisory workflow validation.
- A one-page MOU/pilot brief is ready.
- External outreach and outcome tracking are moved to follow-up issues.

## Pilot Scope

Allowed:

- public-data-only Singapore entity and sector-module checks;
- demo data, synthetic entities, or public organizations approved for review;
- dossier exports, provenance/freshness/gaps/limits review, and analyst handoff;
- feedback on workflow clarity, auditability, and enterprise buyer objections.

Not allowed:

- claims that Dude performs AML, sanctions, legal, tax, credit, investment, audit, or licensed compliance decisions;
- confidential client data without hosted beta controls, DPA review, retention policy, and support owner;
- private ACRA enrichment, ownership graphs, or paid-data redistribution without source permission;
- public logo/reference use without written approval.

## One-Page Pilot Brief

Title: Public-data CDD evidence packs for Singapore client onboarding

Problem: Corp-services and diligence teams repeatedly collect public registry evidence but lose provenance, freshness, and gap context across screenshots, spreadsheets, and manual handoffs.

Pilot: In one workshop, Dude produces source-backed dossiers for three approved Singapore entity scenarios and exports evidence packs with explicit gaps and limits.

Success criteria:

- participants can trace each fact to a public source or gap;
- skipped modules are understandable and actionable;
- export artifacts are useful for analyst handoff;
- non-advice boundaries are clear enough for risk/compliance reviewers;
- top five enterprise blockers are recorded as GitHub issues.

Deliverables:

- one workshop deck or agenda;
- three approved test scenarios;
- exported dossier evidence packs;
- blocker/issues list;
- go/no-go note for a deeper private beta or integration review.
