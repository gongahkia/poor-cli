# Accessibility Planning Review

Project: Demo Apartment

Profile: wheelchair / caregiver-assisted planning screen

## Summary

Haus flags route blockers, doorway targets, turning-circle risks, trip hazards, and bathroom/kitchen approach notes from the current layout.

## Review Buckets

- Quick wins: remove trip hazards, improve lighting, clear night routes.
- Move furniture: restore circulation and transfer space.
- Renovate: widen narrow openings or change door swings only after professional review.
- Verify on site: confirm measurements before relying on the recommendations.

Accessibility output is planning guidance only, not ADA certification, medical advice, or an occupational therapy assessment.
