# Furniture Fit Report

Project: Demo Apartment

Product: Oversized sofa

## Fit Result

Status: fails

Haus compares product dimensions, clearance needs, room bounds, and delivery assumptions before purchase.

## Substitute Flow

- Smaller product suggestions are listed when the chosen item fails.
- Shopping-list CSV exports product name, dimensions, quantity, source URL, and fit notes.
- "Buy nothing yet" appears when scale, doorway width, or product dimensions are unconfirmed.

## Measure Before Buying

- Entry and room doorway clear width.
- Product boxed dimensions and assembled dimensions.
- Walkway and pull-out clearance where the product will be used.
