# Designer Pre-Sales Pack

Project: Demo Client Apartment

Client: Avery Client

## Client Brief

Goals, constraints, assumptions, unanswered questions, and selected scenario are captured as a client-safe brief.

## Lead Qualification

- Client needs: storage, practical circulation, low-renovation refresh.
- Spatial risks: unverified scale, missing opening widths, fixture constraints.
- Likely scope: concept plan, validation notes, optional product sourcing.
- Follow-up questions: measurements, preferences, budget, routines, constraints.

## Deliverables

- Branded static HTML report.
- Proposal outline.
- Client questions export.
- Design call script.
- Presentation mode for screen sharing.
